Minifig Facts

The LEGO "Scout Trooper Episode 3" minifigure, featuring the Kashyyyk Trooper design, is patterned after the Imperial scout troopers from "Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith." This minifigure includes detailed camouflage armor designed to blend into the forested environment of Kashyyyk, the Wookiee home planet. It typically comes with a blaster pistol accessory and a helmet with a visor. This variant was released as part of the Star Wars LEGO sets aimed at recreating scenes from the movie's Battle of Kashyyyk.
