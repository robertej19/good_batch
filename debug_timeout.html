Minifig Facts

Clone Trooper Commander Fox is a LEGO minifigure that appears in the Star Wars theme. He is depicted in his Phase 1 Coruscant Guard uniform, which includes a dark bluish gray visor, pauldron, and kama. The minifigure also features large eyes and lacks a solid light bluish gray semicircle above the belt. Commander Fox served the Galactic Republic and was in charge of maintaining order on Coruscant.
Common description: Clone Trooper Commander Fox, Coruscant Guard (Phase 1) - Dark Bluish Gray Visor, Pauldron, and Kama, Large Eyes, without Solid Light Bluish Gray Semicircle above Belt
